About MyPal

MyPal was created by Laahini A as a hackathon project and initiative during the COVID-19 pandemic. Like millions of other people around the glob, she faced struggles with maintaining a healthy lifestyle while stuck at home. To combat these changes, she created MyPal to keep herself and others motivated and active. Motivation and mental health is also a struggle, causing her to develop sections for goal-keeping and more.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help people around the globe improve their health and wellness during this COVID-19 pandemic. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle is very important, especially during a time of isolation and lack of physical interaction. MyPal aims to guide people in their health journey by helping them achieve their goals. We will always support those who would like to improve mental and physical help. We want to achieve a growing community of like-minded individuals that all aim to sustain their health and well-being.
