YOGA & MEDITATION

You may have noticed the craze about yoga before but you might wonder, what's so cool about it. Yoga is a physical exercise that requires breath control. relaxation, diet control, positive thinking and meditation. The goal is to create a state of harmony and connection between the body, mid and environment. This ancient practice originates from South Asia and has several prove health benefits. Yoga is not extremely physically demanding but the intensity can increase as you progress in your journey. It is perfect to do at home and with others because it requires very little equipment, just a yoga mat and some workout clothes!
As mentioned, there are several physical and mental benefits of yoga. Yoga helps relax your mind as it requires you to clear your thoughts and meditate to focus on becoming one with yourself. According to studies, yoga also helps you become a mindful eater and aids in weight loss/maintenance. Furthermore, it assists in improving your breathing which will greatly help with cardiovascular fitness. It also helps you soothe anxiety and releive you of stress. Doing yoga daily or even weekly is highly beneficial in getting some movement activity while staying at home(although you can do it outside too!).

Other Resources

  • OK2TALK

    OK2TALK is a community that encourages teens and young adults struggling with mental health problems to share their personal stories of recovery, tragedy, struggle or hope using any medium of creative content.

  • Crisis Text Line

    Crisis Text line is a 24-hour support for those in crisis. Text 741741 from anywhere in the US and you will receive an automated text asking you what your crisis is. Within minutes, a live trained crisis counselor will answer your text for free.

  • Calm

    Calm is a website that assists you in relieving stress through various activities such as meditation with different voices and guided practice.

  • Exhaler

    Exhaler is a free website that helps you practice breathing techniques for relaxation and managing anxiety. It can help clear your mind and also provide respiratory improvement.
